Alfredo Pasta

Description:

Alfredo Pasta is a recipe that invites a lot of discussion in simple terms, it's a silky egg pasta smothered with melted butter and parmigiano cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Romano Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • garlic salt

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
