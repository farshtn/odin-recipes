Lasagna

Description:

Learn how to make a lasagna that rivals the best Italian restaurants, with layers of savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • parsley
  • Italian seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • mozzarella Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the Cheeses mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
