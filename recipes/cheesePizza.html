Cheese Pizza

Description:

How to make a cheese pizza at home that will be better than anything from the delivery driver. Perfect for families with kids or pizza lovers who appreciate high quality ingredients in an easy recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Olive Oil
  • Canned Tomato Sauce
  • Italian Herbs
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Garlic Salt
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Roll your pizza dough out by hand to a 12-inch circle.
  2. Brush olive oil all along the outer edge to help make a golden brown crust.
  3. Spread the marinara sauce over the top of the pizza.
  4. tear the provolone slices and scatter them over the top.
  5. Shred the mozzarella and cover the entire tomato sauce part.
